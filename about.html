Our Mission

Operation Sindoor is a website created to honour the bravery and sacrifice of Indian soldiers. It aims to present the story of the operation with respect and accuracy while focusing on human courage.

What This Project Represents

This project is a student-led presentation combining research, visuals and storytelling to educate visitors about the operation.
